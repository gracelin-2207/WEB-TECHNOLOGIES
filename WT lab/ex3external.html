Click a mood to explore visual inspiration + pro tips

🌿 Nature Photography

📍 Best Locations: Forests, mountains, riversides, gardens

☀️ Best Lighting Time: Golden hour (sunrise or sunset)

🏙️ Urban Photography

📍 Best Locations: City streets, architecture, cafes, rooftops

☀️ Best Lighting Time: Blue hour (just after sunset) or mid-morning

🧑 Portrait Photography

📍 Best Locations: Studios, natural window light, outdoor parks

☀️ Best Lighting Time: Soft daylight or cloudy weather for even tones

🌌 Night Photography

📍 Best Locations: City skylines, starry skies, neon-lit streets

☀️ Best Lighting Time: After sunset with long exposure
